Spotify Help

Support Center

Welcome to the Spotify Support Center! Here you can find answers to common questions about our service. If you need further assistance, feel free to chat with us or visit our community.

How do I reset my password?
To reset your password, go to the login page and click on "Forgot your password?" Follow the instructions to reset it via email.
How can I update my payment method?
You can update your payment method by going to your account settings and selecting "Payment methods". Choose the method you want to update and follow the prompts.
How do I cancel my subscription?
To cancel your subscription, log into your account, go to "Subscription" settings, and click "Cancel Subscription". Follow the steps to confirm.
What are the available subscription plans?
Spotify offers various subscription plans including Free, Premium Individual, Duo, Family, and Student plans. Each plan offers different features to suit your needs.
